BKIM MUSIC PRODUCTIONS

Original Compositions, Digital Production, Mixing


Hi, my name is Brian Kim. When I picked up the piano back agin after 8 years just to play the main theme music of the HBO show Westworld, I knew my musical journey had begun. After practicing on the piano with existing pieces of classical music, I began to wonder if I could write music of my own. This small persuit of mine to express myself with music has developed through the years to become an integral part of my identity. I hope you enjoy my compositions!
