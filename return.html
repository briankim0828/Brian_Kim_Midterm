return (Summer);

Composed at my internship at KRUST, Summer of 2021, with the help of Krust's Music AI

Medium: digital music production (FL Studio)

genre: pop/acoustic beat

At my internship at a AI startup called KRUST, aside from learning about the basics of AI and machine learning, I also had the chance to play around with the product their were developing. The musical AI was trained on a list of successful music in Korea. It could generate a basic melody and some chords, which I could import into my DAW (Digital Audio Workstation) to compose and mix it to my liking.

The chords that I chose were brilliant: a mix of happiness, melancholy, and relaxation. The melody, however, was not as catchy and iconic as I wanted it to be. I mixed up some of the notes, added some syncopation and out came a relaxed, summer day beat. To honor my digital collaborative partner, I named the piece "return (Summer);" to symbolize the lines of code that helped me with the inspiration for the piece.
