Long_Night_Ahead

Composed at my home in Seoul, Summer of 2021

Medium: digital music production (FL Studio)

genre: ambient, atmospheric music

After my composing "Holden's Theme," I never really had the chance to compose another finished piece. I was always on the piano, coming up with interesting musical ideas, but I never had the outlet to create a cohesive track with my ideas. Then, after I began to learn digital music production, I found a tool to combine all of my ideas and give them character through sound design and mixing with the audio itself. I became fascinated, and began to work on my second composition.

After watching "The Social Network," I was mezmerized by Trent Reznor's distorted, electronic soundtrack. In order to replicate such a sound with my personal flair, I added a simple piano line to tie the piece back to my musical roots. I then combined it with some base guitar lines, drums, and synthesizers to create the feeling you get when you're pulling an all-nighter, working on homework or a project, hence: Long Night Ahead.
